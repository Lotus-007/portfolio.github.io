PSYCHO Project

Description

This is a photobook I made, taking the subject of mental violence. This project shows the mental aspects of the victim under the influence of mental violence, thus warning people of its seriousness in life, often overlooked.

As we all know, there are two types of violence, physical violence and mental violence. in which mental violence is much more dangerous because of its unexpression .

Unlike physical violence, it leaves wounds, while mental violence causes mental breakdown, leaving victims traumatized without leaving any trace, but slowly destroying the inside.
