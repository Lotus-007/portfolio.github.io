Son Tinh, Thuy Tinh Project

Description

This is a project I implemented during the 2D animation semester. It is based on the Vietnamese legend of the war between the mountain god and the sea god. But I changed
the original a bit to make it more interesting for the viewers. I mainly show you about character design and background. These are the 2 parts I like the best in this project.

Characters

In character design part, I show you three main characters:
  • My Nuong - the Princess
  • Son tinh - the Mountain-God
  • Thuy Tinh - the Sea-God
  • Hung Vuong - the King
I have researched many documents to create characters with ancient costumes of Vietnam. In particular, the two characters Son Tinh and Thuy Tinh have the shape of animals such as shark and ape, to create the special of my story.

Background

In this section, I used the technique of layer separation for
each background to create a near-far effect when moving.
As is known, if only the character moves in a normal back ground, each movement that the machine takes will look
more fake, making the movie become less attractive.
Because in fact, when moving the camera, things farther away will move differently than things closer. Therefore, to create depth for the space, the separation of layers is really necessary.
